Kelly Rich Family Medicine

The Heart of Family Medicine

Kelly Rich Family Medicine was established in 2016 as a solo provider medical clinic with an excellent staff and a common vision of high quality care.

Kelly Rich MD did her undergraduate work at the Georgia Institute of Technology, received her medical doctorate from the Medical College of Georgia and did internship and residency at the Atlanta Medical Center Family Medicine Residency Program. She has lived in Georgia her entire life and has practiced medicine here since 2001. Her residency program invited her back to teach up and coming doctors the art and philosophy of Family Medicine and she now wants to get back to full time patient care. She is board certified through the American Board of Family Medicine, licensed through the Georgia Composite Medical Board and an American Academy of Family Physicians member.
